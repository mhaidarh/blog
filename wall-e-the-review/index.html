WALL·E: The Review

Mar 19th, 2014 Future, HCI, Review, Robot, Technology Mar 19th, 2014

Intro

  • World

  • Set in the distant future.

  • Total dystopian future on Earth, even on Earth’s Moon.

  • Kind of utopian future in space (the Axiom)

  • Story

    • WALL•E, the movie’s name.
    • Science fiction.

Characters

  • WALL•E (Waste Allocation Load Lifter: Earth-Class) Wheeler-type robot

  • EVE (Extraterrestrial Vegetation Evaluator) Hover probe-type robot

  • Both of these characters like resemble Adam and Eve.

  • Various Robots

  • Beyond artificial intelligence (AI)

    • Almost all machines are all automatically controlled.
    • The robots could move smoothly, think, express, behave, sense, feel, and even love like human. Also, have a specific gender and sound with half speaking ability.
    • Although most of the robots are not humanoid.

Interaction

WALL•E with

EVE with her scanning sensor/device.

Mostly with voice command

TL;DR

The interaction between human and robots in the future, based on WALL-E movie.
